LEGO Assembly
A Porshe 911 modeled based on measurements
from LEGO bricks. The model is very close to a
1 : 1 scale, with afew values rounded for
consistiency between similar pieces. The project
consists of about 50 seperate parts, taking well
over 30 hours to complete and assemble each
part.

Vice Assembly
 A fully functional vice, modeled to mirror that of
a standard, mountable vice grip. This was a
class-wide project, which I completed within two
hours of its release to students. Each assembled
part moves as it would in life, thanks to
constraints and tolerances to control the
movement.

Midterm Assembly 
This assembly was a class-wide project to test
our abilities as a midterm assignment. Given
3 days to complete the parts and assembly,
I completed the project a while earlier. Some
instructions were unclear throughout, but I
finished the assignment with plenty of ease.

Lego Tire 
While tire is also featured in the LEGO Assembly, I chose to go further into the character
of it. I customized the textures, added bump
maps to create the realisic lighting, and altered
the lighting to optimize the detail. Overall, the
detailing on the tire was an addional 5 hours
to the project.
